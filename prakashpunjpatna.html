Prakash Punj, Patna - Prefabricated Sewage Treatment Plant (STP)

Shivaur designed and installed the 100 KLD Prefabricated STP at Prakash Punj, Patna. This project is a testimony to a compact, efficient solution that is turnkey and ready to deploy for sewage treatment. The prefabricated STP at Prakash Punj is a live example of rapid deployment and efficiency in urban settings, which has always looked at saving space and time. On a general note, the project testifies to how modern technologies have been stitched into flexible, scalable solutions that change incrementally with the growth in urban population needs.

  • Chemical-free biofiltration to reduce reliance on harsh treatment methods.
  • Noise reduction enclosures around mechanical equipment to minimize acoustic impact.
  • ZExpandable modules to easily increase capacity as demand grows.
  • Heat recovery systems to utilize thermal energy from sewage treatment processes.

The STP addresses the immediate need for the efficient management of sewage. Still, it would also fit in with existing urban infrastructure and maximize the best use of available space. This project displays Shivaur's innovation in developing solutions for environmental problems and serves as an example for replication in other such urban settings worldwide.
