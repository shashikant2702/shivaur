Bringing expertise to every project

Instapower has developed the technology behind LED-lighting systems after investing many man-years in R&D, mastering the technology – electronics and optics. Instapower and its founder have filed more than 90 patents in India and abroad in the area of LED lighting and energy efficiency. LED’s are incredibly long lasting, energy efficient, virtually unbreakable, versatile, directional and, most important of all, environmentally friendly, saving emissions of millions of tons of carbon dioxide into the atmosphere from use of incandescent bulbs which only convert about 5% of electrical energy to light, wasting the rest. Instapower has filed more than 70 patents in India & abroad in the area of LED lighting.

We have been recognized by the Ministry of Science and Technology, Government of India as a R&D lab having a dedicated facility and team working on new and innovative products for energy conservation.
