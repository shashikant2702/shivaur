Kirloskar Pumps

At Shivaur, we offer a wide range of Kirloskar pumps, which fulfill every demand from industries, commerce, and residential sectors. Accordingly, our Borewell Oil-filled Submersible Pumps and Borewell Water-filled Submersible Pumps are designed to perform consistently under harsh conditions at the bottom of borewells. The End-Suction Pumps are suited for versatile applications in fluid handling, thus assuring efficient and long-lasting operation. Our jet pumps ensure peak performance for high-pressure water delivery, while monobloc pumps ensure compact construction for easy installation and maintenance. Openwell Submersible Pumps cater to open-well water extraction, providing consistent water flow.

Our self-priming application pumps are perfect for immediate and effortless start-ups. Solar pumping systems provide sustainable, energy-efficient solutions to water supply. In applications that call for air and gas removal, vacuum pumps will be appropriate, while vertical multistage pumps should be used in applications calling for multi-stage pressure increase. Shivaur also deals with a series of fire fighting pumps developed to conform to safety standards and provide reliable fire protection. Mini Pumps are compact solutions for application on a small scale, while Multi-Stage Pumps offer high efficiency in handling fluids across several stages. Our Pressure Boosting Pumps ensure continuous water pressure in residential and commercial areas.
