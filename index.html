about us

Shivaur: Excellence in Pumping & Water Treatment

Shivaur was established in 2011 with the commitment of providing high-quality water pumping and treatment solutions. The firm is registered with many organizations and has completed many projects offered by the public and private sectors.

We have expertise in executing SITC (supply, installation, testing, and commissioning) and EPC (engineering, procurement, and construction) projects with a specification in STP (sewage treatment plant), WTP (water treatment plant), hydropneumatic systems, etc.

With an emphasis on institutional sales, we have the infrastructure for placing and executing the turnkey projects from designing to successful commissioning.
