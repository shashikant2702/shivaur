Solar Pumping Plant

What It Is:

A set of solar panels and solar-powered pumps for water supply systems in most rural and off-grid areas.

How It's Done:

Starting with a site assessment to determine the optimal angle for maximum energy capture, the system is designed with solar panels, an inverter, and a solar pump.
